The Organic Performance
Observation is
an observation
of how each
Organic Performance
Collection is
either Organic
or Inorganic
by assessing
their overall state.

The Equilibrium
is the collective
equator for the
weight of
Organic States.

Each Collection
Category contains
information about
the Organic State,
being either Organic
or Inorganic.
For the Organic State,
an Organic
State means
that the Collection
is determining
that the person
or place is
alive and growing
in a healthy
way within
the measures
of that category.

An Inorganic State
is a determination
that the person
or place is
dying in
that category
of determination.

Each Data Pebble,
an item of
information
collecting data
about a topic,
should be
considered as
a weight leaning
towards either
life or death.
In this way,
we can observe
if a Collection
is Organic
or Inorganic.

The Equilibrium
is determined by
Boem's program,
autonomously.

Export each
Observation in
the same way
as that of
the Collections,
on their
Exporter page.
