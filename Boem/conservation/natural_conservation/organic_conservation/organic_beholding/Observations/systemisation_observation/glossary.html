Glossary

Observation Categorisation

The observation information for that particular observation.

Observation Date

The date of the observation process.

Collection Name

The name best representing the collection for that person or place.

Observation Count

What count of observation this is for that person or place.

Systemisation Observation

The observation for how an item is retaining someone or somewhere into a System.

Systemic Application

The usage for an item within a System.

To request help with an Observation, email forestvaneeden@icloud.com a copy of your collection with "Observation Request" in your subject line.
