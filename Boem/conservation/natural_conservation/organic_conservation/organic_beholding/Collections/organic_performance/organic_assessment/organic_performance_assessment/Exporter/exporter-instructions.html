To save your
Collections, please,
export each Collection
as a separate PDF file.

This can be done by
opening the File tab
inside your menu bar,
and then open the
Export to PDF
window to save.

On iPhone or iPad,
you can save your
Collections, by
pressing the "Share"
button, and then,
selecting "Web Archive"
in Options.

Another smartphone
option is to
save as PDF
during Print.

Save your new
Collections into
a Collected folder
on your device.
