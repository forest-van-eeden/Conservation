A Systemisation Level
Assessment is
a form of Collection
that shows the
level of
Systemisation
that an item, needed
by a person
or place, has.

This helps to
build an understanding
for how
desystemisation
could begin
to take place.
Desystemisation
is the process
for someone
or somewhere
to take so
that they can
remain in
Organic Experience.

The Systemisation
Level Assessment
is taken in
the same manner
as the Organic
Performance
Assessment.
The Export
procedure is
also the same.
A separate
Systemisation Levels
Assessments
folder can be
made in order
to organise
these Collections.

Each level is
given per
input item.
Item details
can benefit
being given
date of release,
and name.
