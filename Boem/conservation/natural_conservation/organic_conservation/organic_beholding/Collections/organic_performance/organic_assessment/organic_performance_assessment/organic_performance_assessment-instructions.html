This assessment has
a categorisation area
at the beginning
where you can name
your Collection and
keep count of
how many that person
or place has.
In the assessment
you can then
mark present
categories and
give an amount
and explanation.

It is recommended
to Capitalise
words in your
explanations that
can help Boem
to link your
words to what
Organic Needs
you may have.

Organic Needs
are needs that
exist in order
for life
to continue.

Once you have
filled out
a category pebble
you can save
it to the Exporter
by pressing the
save button.

You can then export
your Collections
and save them
into the Collected
folder inside of
the Collections folder.
