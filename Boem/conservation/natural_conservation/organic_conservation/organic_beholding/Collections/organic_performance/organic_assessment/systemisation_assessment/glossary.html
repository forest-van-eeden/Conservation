Glossary

Systemisation Level

The level of intensity for how an item is retaining someone or somewhere into a System.

Wild

This level of systemisation means that the person or place is nude in having any systemisation.

Domestic

Meaning that the person or place is able to reach homesteads with that item.

Civilian

Items that connect to or are requirements in order to be a part of a country's grid.

Militarised

Items requiring a level of clearance, within a Governing System, in order to use and obtain.

Non-Existential

Items that make individuals or places perceived as not existing to the naked senses.

Autoriteit

Items that retain someone or somewhere into being operated by a System.
