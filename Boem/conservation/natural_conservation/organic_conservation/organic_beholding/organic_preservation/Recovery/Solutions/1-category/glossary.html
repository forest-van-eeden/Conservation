Glossary

Category

A category meant to be within your problem, to begin to build complexity needed for your Solution.

Problem Name

The name of the problem, beginning to categorise the problem within Solutions that has already been created in the Problem step..

Collection Name

The name of the Collection collected, previously, for that person or place.

Observation Count

What count of Observation that was for that person or place.

Problem Collection

The Collection that is containing the Problem.

Category Name

The name representing the Category you are describing within the Problem.

Research

What information you have begun to find for your Solution.

Knowledge

The prior or new knowledge that you have for your Solution.
