When we go
through the Problem
process, we begin
to identify our
problems that have
been noted during the
Collections process.

Beginning with
reviewing the Inorganic
equilibriums within
your Observations,
use the Problem
process to identify
your problems that
are causing your
Organic States
to become Inorganic.

Go deeper into
your Observations to
view which Pebbles
have Inorganic States.

From there, review
your Collections
to understand
the causes for
the Inorganic Observations.

When choosing your
Problem's Collection,
try and choose
the one that
encompasses the
recognised Problem
as much as possible.

The Cause
is where you can
explain how your
problem began
or is occurring.

The Result
is what is occurring
because of your
problem.
