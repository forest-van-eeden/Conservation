A Solution is
when we put
together all
of our Problem,
Category, and
finally build
the complete Solution.

The Solution
is the final
step in Solutions
to make your idea.
From this point,
after, we begin
to Recover with
the Solution made.

Like a cleansing
tea, your Solution
helps others Recover.

Just like in
the Problem and
Category steps,
in the Solution,
there are two
areas to complete.
The first is Ideas
and the second
is Possibilities.

Ideas are the
thoughts that you have
for how to
fix your problem.
You may be able
to use your Category
Export to find your
ideas.

Possibilities are what
is real in
your ideas that
has a way to
actually happen.
These are where
the ideas have
possibility in life.

Export your Solution
in the same way
as your Problem,
and Category.
