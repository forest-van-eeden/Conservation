Organic Gardening

Organic Gardening is
the thought for people
to know the effect
of what they eat
on soil.

This is where it begins.

Naturally, humanity
makes soil with seeds.
We go about the Earth
planting seeds from
our soil.

The main concern here,
in Conservation,
is that there are
no remains in the
soil from mankind.

The reason for this
is because the soil
from mankind with remains
is unnatural for
plants to learn to
grow in properly.

It causes issues like
cancerous masses
and lumping clumps
to come out of the
growth from the plants
causing ailments.
